Some of the Ways I Help

I’ll take wonderful care of your children while you’re away and make sure they’re nourished and loved. No matter how old your child is, I have years of experience working with children of all ages. I provide flexible, safe, fun, and nurturing childcare options at competitive rates. Contact me today to learn more about my services.

We provide a warm, supportive, and nurturing educational environment in our preschool / daycare program for children ages 12 months to 5 years. Students enjoy a kindergarten readiness curriculum that includes early literacy, math, science, language, art, music, dance, and sports.
